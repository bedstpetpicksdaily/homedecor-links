Frequently Asked Questions

What size Moroccan rug is best for a living room?

A 5x7 or 8x10 Moroccan area rug is ideal for most living rooms, providing enough coverage to anchor furniture while maintaining visual balance in the space.

Are wool Moroccan rugs suitable for families with pets?

Yes, wool Moroccan rugs are pet-friendly and durable. Wool naturally resists stains and is easy to vacuum. Regular maintenance helps preserve the rug's appearance and longevity.

How do I style a Moroccan area rug with other home décor?

Pair your Moroccan rug with complementary throw pillows, decorative lamps, and curtains in coordinating colors. Layering textures creates visual depth while maintaining a cohesive design aesthetic.

What's the difference between authentic and machine-made Moroccan rugs?

Authentic hand-woven Moroccan rugs feature unique variations and superior craftsmanship, while machine-made versions offer affordability and consistency. Wool construction is available in both styles.
