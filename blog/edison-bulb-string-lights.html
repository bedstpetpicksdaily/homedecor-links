Frequently Asked Questions

Are Edison bulb string lights energy efficient?

Yes, LED Edison bulb string lights are highly energy efficient, consuming significantly less electricity than traditional incandescent options while providing the same warm, vintage aesthetic.

Can I use Edison string lights indoors and outdoors?

Many Edison bulb string lights are rated for both indoor and outdoor use. Always check the product specifications to ensure weather resistance and proper installation for your intended location.

How long do Edison bulb string lights last?

LED Edison bulbs typically last 25,000-50,000 hours, while traditional incandescent bulbs last 1,500-3,000 hours. LED options offer better longevity and durability.

What color temperature works best for home decor?

Warm white (2700K-3000K) Edison bulbs are ideal for home decor as they create a cozy, inviting atmosphere that complements furniture, rugs, and wall art.
