Frequently Asked Questions

What size room is best for a 5x7 area rug?

A 5x7 area rug works well in living rooms, master bedrooms, and dining areas. It's large enough to anchor furniture while fitting comfortably in most residential spaces.

How do I clean a modern geometric area rug?

Most geometric rugs can be vacuumed regularly and spot-cleaned with mild soap and water. Check the manufacturer's care instructions for specific material recommendations.

What colors work best with geometric rugs?

Geometric rugs pair beautifully with neutral throw pillows, solid-colored curtains, and minimalist wall art. Bold geometric patterns work as statement pieces, while subtle designs complement existing decor.

Are geometric rugs suitable for high-traffic areas?

Yes, quality modern geometric rugs made from durable materials like polypropylene or wool are excellent for entryways and living rooms with regular foot traffic.
