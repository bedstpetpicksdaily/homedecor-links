Frequently Asked Questions

How does the rust orange color look in different lighting?

Rust orange deepens in warm artificial light and appears more muted in bright natural daylight. North-facing rooms will show cooler undertones while south-facing spaces enhance the warmth. Check customer photos in verified reviews showing the rug in actual homes rather than staging photos.

Is this rug suitable for pet owners?

The Aztec Trellis pattern's geometric design helps disguise light pet hair and minor stains, but the rust tone shows dust and debris more readily than neutral colors. Vacuum weekly if you have shedding pets. Some reviewers report the rug holds odors if accidents occur—address spills immediately with enzymatic cleaner.

What kind of underpad do I need?

Use a non-slip rubber-backed underpad rated for hard and soft floors depending on your subfloor type. Thick felt pads (3/8-inch) work best for this weight and size to prevent bunching during foot traffic and protect hardwood underneath.

Can I use this in a modern or minimalist space?

The strong tribal aesthetic limits versatility outside southwestern and bohemian decorating styles. Modern and minimalist spaces typically clash with the Aztec Trellis pattern unless you're specifically designing around it as an intentional focal point.
