Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes of ceramic vases are available?

Ceramic vases come in various sizes ranging from small 4-inch accent pieces to tall 12+ inch floor vases. Choose based on your space and display needs.

How do I style ceramic vases with other home decor?

Pair vases with your existing curtains, rugs, and throw pillows by matching colors or complementary tones. Group odd numbers together for visual appeal.

Are ceramic vases easy to clean?

Yes, most ceramic vases can be dusted regularly and wiped with a damp cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals that may damage the finish.

Can ceramic vases hold fresh flowers?

Many ceramic vases are waterproof and suitable for fresh flowers. Check product specifications to ensure water-holding capability before use.
