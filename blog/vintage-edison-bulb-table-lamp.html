Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Edison bulb?

An Edison bulb is a vintage-style light bulb featuring a visible filament design, named after Thomas Edison. Modern versions combine this retro aesthetic with LED technology for energy efficiency and longevity.

Are Edison bulb table lamps bright enough for reading?

Most Edison bulbs provide warm, ambient lighting ideal for mood setting. For reading, consider pairing your Edison lamp with additional overhead lighting or choosing higher-wattage LED versions.

What decor styles work with Edison bulb lamps?

Edison bulbs complement industrial, vintage, steampunk, farmhouse, and modern eclectic designs. Their warm glow works beautifully in bedrooms, living rooms, home offices, and entryways.

How long do LED Edison bulbs last?

Quality LED Edison bulbs typically last 25,000 to 50,000 hours, making them a cost-effective lighting solution that reduces frequent replacements compared to incandescent options.
