Frequently Asked Questions

What size should I choose for my living room?

For most living rooms, a 5x7 or 5x8 Moroccan Trellis Area Rug works best. This size anchors seating arrangements while leaving room for walking space around edges. Measure your room and furniture placement before purchasing.

Is the non-slip backing effective on hardwood floors?

Yes, the non-slip backing provides excellent grip on hardwood, tile, and laminate floors. However, it's still recommended to use a rug pad underneath for added stability and protection of your flooring.

How do I clean and maintain this geometric area rug?

Vacuum regularly with a soft-bristle brush to prevent dust accumulation. For spills, blot immediately with a damp cloth. Professional cleaning is recommended annually for deep cleaning and to extend the rug's lifespan.

What colors does the Moroccan Trellis pattern come in?

This popular geometric rug is available in various colorways including grey, blue, beige, and navy options to complement different home décor styles and color schemes.
