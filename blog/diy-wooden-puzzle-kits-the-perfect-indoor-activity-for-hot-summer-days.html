Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups are EWA Eco-Wood-Art puzzle kits suitable for?

EWA offers kits ranging from beginner-level (ages 8+) to advanced (adult collectors). Each kit clearly indicates recommended age and skill level, ensuring you choose appropriately challenging projects for different family members.

How long does it typically take to complete a wooden puzzle kit?

Completion time varies by project complexity, typically ranging from 4-20 hours. Simple decorative models may take 4-6 hours, while intricate mechanical STEM models can require 15-20+ hours of engaging work—perfect for spreading across multiple summer days.

Are EWA Eco-Wood-Art kits truly eco-friendly and sustainably sourced?

Yes, EWA prioritizes sustainable practices by using responsibly sourced wood and non-toxic materials. All kits are designed to create lasting pieces that serve as permanent home décor, maximizing their environmental value over their lifetime.

Most EWA kits require no special tools—just the small tools included in the package. Some advanced models may benefit from having a hobby knife or sandpaper available, but these are optional for enhanced finishing rather than essential for assembly.

Can wooden puzzle kits be good gifts for summer visitors?

Absolutely! EWA's beautifully packaged kits make excellent gifts. They provide both entertainment during visits and lasting keepsakes guests can take home—or they can enjoy building together as a memorable summer bonding activity.
