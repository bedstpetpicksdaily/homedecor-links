Frequently Asked Questions

Do 84 inch blackout curtains fit all window sizes?

84 inch curtains are ideal for standard bedroom windows and sliding glass doors. For wider windows, consider purchasing multiple panels or measuring your window width before ordering to ensure proper coverage.

Can blackout curtains really reduce outside noise?

Yes, thermal blackout curtains with dense, heavy fabrics can significantly reduce outside noise by absorbing sound waves. The thicker the material, the better the noise reduction capabilities.

How do I care for 84 inch blackout curtains?

Most blackout curtains are machine washable in cold water on a gentle cycle. Check the manufacturer's care instructions, avoid high heat drying, and hang to dry to maintain the blackout coating and fabric integrity.

What colors are best for blackout curtains?

Darker colors like black, dark gray, and deep navy provide maximum light blockage and complement most bedroom decor. However, lighter neutral tones are also available and pair well with contemporary interior design styles.
