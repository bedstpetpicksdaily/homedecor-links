Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Nuloom Aztec Trellis rug suitable for high-traffic areas?

Yes, hand-knotted wool rugs are highly durable and suitable for high-traffic areas. However, regular maintenance and professional cleaning will help extend its lifespan.

What size options are available for this Nuloom rug?

The Nuloom Aztec Trellis comes in multiple sizes, including 8x10, making it adaptable to various room dimensions and layouts.

How do I care for a hand-knotted wool area rug?

Vacuum regularly with a brush attachment, rotate the rug quarterly, and schedule professional cleaning annually to maintain its beauty and durability.

Does wool naturally resist stains?

Yes, wool has natural stain-resistant properties due to its waxy coating, making it easier to clean than many synthetic alternatives.
