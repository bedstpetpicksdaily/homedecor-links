Can I install this myself without an electrician?

Most homeowners can, yes—if your home has existing light fixture wiring and you're comfortable with basic electrical safety (turning off breakers, testing for live wires). The Schoolhouse Electric pendant typically requires mounting the canopy, connecting three wires, and securing the fixture. Budget 30–45 minutes. Will the brass tarnish or require constant polishing?

Yes, it will develop patina—that's the aesthetic appeal for most buyers. You don't need to polish it unless you prefer shiny brass. Some people intentionally accelerate the patina with vinegar because the aged look is genuinely more sophisticated than raw brass. This isn't a maintenance headache; it's a feature, not a bug.
