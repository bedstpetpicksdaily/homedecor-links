Frequently Asked Questions

Are Simple Project macerating toilets reliable for everyday use?

Yes, Simple Project macerating toilets are engineered for residential durability and daily reliability. They feature robust grinding mechanisms and are designed to handle standard bathroom usage without issues.

Can I install a Simple Project smart toilet myself?

While some installation steps can be DIY, smart toilets typically require professional electrical work. Simple Project provides detailed instructions, and professional plumbers are familiar with their systems.

What makes Simple Project macerating toilets better for basements?

Simple Project macerating toilets don't require the waste line to slope downward toward the septic or sewer system, making them perfect for basements where traditional gravity-fed plumbing is impossible.
