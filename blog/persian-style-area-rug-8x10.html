Frequently Asked Questions

What size room is best for an 8x10 Persian area rug?

An 8x10 rug is ideal for large living rooms, spacious bedrooms, or dining areas. It works best in rooms that are at least 12x14 feet, allowing the rug to anchor furniture while leaving 18-24 inches of flooring visible around the edges.

How do I style a Persian rug with other home décor accents?

Pull accent colors from your Persian rug's palette for throw pillows and curtains. Coordinate wall art in complementary frames, and choose lamp bases in brass, wood, or ceramic that echo the rug's traditional aesthetic for a cohesive, designer look.

Are Persian style rugs suitable for high-traffic areas?

Yes, quality Persian style rugs with non-slip backing are durable enough for living rooms and bedrooms. However, they're best avoided in kitchens or mudrooms where spills are more likely. Regular vacuuming and prompt spot-cleaning will maintain their appearance.

What's the difference between authentic and Persian style rugs?

Authentic Persian rugs are handwoven in Iran using traditional methods, while Persian style rugs are inspired designs made with modern materials. Both offer beautiful aesthetics, but authentic pieces are investment-quality heirlooms, while style versions are more affordable.
