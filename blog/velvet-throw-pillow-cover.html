Frequently Asked Questions

How do you clean velvet throw pillow covers?

Most velvet pillow covers can be gently vacuumed with a soft brush attachment or lightly brushed to remove dust. For stains, spot-clean with a damp cloth and mild soap, then air dry. Always check the manufacturer's care instructions before cleaning.

What sizes do velvet throw pillow covers come in?

Velvet pillow covers are available in various standard sizes including 12x12, 16x16, 18x18, 20x20, and 22x22 inches. Some retailers offer larger sizes for decorative floor pillows. Check product listings to find your preferred dimensions.

Are velvet pillow covers suitable for all room types?

Yes, velvet pillow covers work well in bedrooms, living rooms, dens, and home offices. However, they may require more maintenance in high-traffic areas or homes with pets. Consider your lifestyle and room usage when choosing velvet.

How do velvet pillows compare to other decorative pillow materials?

Velvet offers superior softness and luxury compared to cotton or linen, but requires more care. Velvet is more durable than silk and offers better value, while providing an upscale appearance that elevates room aesthetics.
