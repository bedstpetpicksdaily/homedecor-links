Frequently Asked Questions

What size Moroccan geometric rug is best for a living room?

An 8x10 area rug is ideal for most living rooms as it anchors furniture groupings and fits well under sofas and coffee tables. For smaller spaces, consider 5x8, and for larger rooms, opt for 9x12 or larger options.

How do I care for a Moroccan geometric area rug?

Vacuum regularly with a low-pile setting, rotate the rug quarterly to prevent uneven wear, and spot-clean spills immediately with a damp cloth. For deep cleaning, use professional rug cleaning services annually.

Are Moroccan geometric rugs suitable for high-traffic areas?

Yes, quality Moroccan geometric rugs are durable and designed to handle high-traffic areas. Look for rugs with tightly woven construction and medium to high pile weight for maximum durability.

What décor styles pair well with Moroccan geometric rugs?

These rugs work beautifully with bohemian, eclectic, mid-century modern, and contemporary styles. They also complement traditional décor and can balance minimalist spaces by adding warmth and pattern.
