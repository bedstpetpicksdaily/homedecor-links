Frequently Asked Questions

What size macramé wall hanging should I choose?

Choose based on wall space: small (18-24") for accent areas, medium (36-48") for focal walls, and large (60"+) for expansive spaces. Measure your wall and furniture proportions before purchasing.

How do I clean and maintain my macramé tapestry?

Gently dust with a soft brush or cloth weekly. Vacuum on low setting monthly. Avoid water and direct sunlight to prevent fading and fiber damage.

Are macramé wall hangings suitable for every room?

Yes, macramé works beautifully in bedrooms, living rooms, entryways, and offices. Avoid high-moisture areas like bathrooms to protect the natural fibers.

What's the difference between handmade and machine-made macramé?

Handmade pieces offer unique character and superior quality but cost more. Machine-made options are affordable and consistent, though less authentic-feeling.
