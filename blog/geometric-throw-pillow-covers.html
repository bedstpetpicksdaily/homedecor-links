Frequently Asked Questions

What size geometric throw pillow cover should I buy?

Standard sizes are 16x16", 18x18", and 20x20". Measure your pillow inserts first, then select a cover size that matches. Slightly oversized covers create a fuller, more polished look.

Are geometric throw pillow covers machine washable?

Most are machine washable on gentle cycle, but always check the care label. Many high-quality covers recommend cold water and air drying to maintain color and shape.

Can I use geometric pillows in any room?

Yes! Geometric patterns work in bedrooms, living rooms, offices, and even patios. Choose neutral tones for traditional spaces or bold colors for contemporary décor.

How do geometric pillows coordinate with other home décor?

Pick one dominant color from your geometric pillow and coordinate with rugs, curtains, and wall art in complementary shades. Mix patterns cautiously to avoid visual clutter.
