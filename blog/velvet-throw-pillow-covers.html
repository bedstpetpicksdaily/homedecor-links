Frequently Asked Questions

Are velvet pillow covers machine washable?

Most velvet pillow covers are spot-clean only or require gentle hand washing. Always check the care label before purchasing, as some premium options may need professional cleaning to preserve their luxurious finish.

What size velvet pillow covers should I buy?

Standard sizes include 16x16, 18x18, and 20x20 inches. Measure your existing pillows or furniture before ordering to ensure proper fit and aesthetic appearance.

Do velvet pillow covers work with modern decor?

Absolutely. Velvet comes in contemporary colors and minimalist styles that complement modern interiors beautifully. Jewel tones, blush, and charcoal velvet covers are popular choices for contemporary home decor.

Can I mix velvet pillows with other fabric types?

Yes, mixing velvet with linen, cotton, and leather creates visual interest and texture contrast. This layering technique is a popular interior design strategy for creating dynamic, inviting spaces.
