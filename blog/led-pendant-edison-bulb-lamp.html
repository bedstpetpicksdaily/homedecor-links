Frequently Asked Questions

Are LED Edison bulbs dimmable?

Many LED Edison bulbs are dimmable, but compatibility depends on your light fixture and dimmer switch. Always check product specifications before purchasing to ensure compatibility.

How long do LED Edison bulbs last?

LED Edison bulbs typically last 25,000 to 50,000 hours, which translates to 10-25 years of regular use, significantly longer than incandescent alternatives.

Can I use LED Edison pendant lights in outdoor spaces?

Some LED Edison pendant lamps are rated for outdoor use, but most are designed for indoor applications. Always verify the product's weather-resistant rating before hanging outdoors.

What color temperature should I choose for my home décor?

Warm white (2700K) creates a cozy ambiance perfect for living areas, while cool white (4000K) works better in modern or contemporary décor settings.
