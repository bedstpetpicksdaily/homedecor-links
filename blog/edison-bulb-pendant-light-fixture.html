Frequently Asked Questions

Are Edison bulb pendant lights suitable for outdoor use?

While some Edison bulb pendant lights are designed for outdoor use, most are intended for indoor spaces. Check the product specifications to ensure weather resistance if you plan to use them on a covered porch or patio.

Can I use LED Edison bulbs in my pendant light fixture?

Yes, most Edison bulb pendant light fixtures are compatible with both traditional and LED Edison bulbs. LED versions offer better energy efficiency and longer lifespan without sacrificing the vintage aesthetic.

What's the typical lifespan of an Edison bulb pendant light?

Traditional Edison bulbs last 1,000-2,000 hours, while LED Edison bulbs can last 15,000-25,000 hours. The fixture itself typically lasts 10+ years with proper care.

How do I install an Edison bulb pendant light?

Most Edison bulb pendant lights follow standard installation procedures. You'll need to turn off power at the breaker, remove the old fixture, connect wires, secure the canopy, and install the bulb. Professional installation is recommended if you're unfamiliar with electrical work.
