Frequently Asked Questions

What size shag area rug should I buy?

Consider your room dimensions and furniture layout. For living rooms, a 5x7 or 8x10 rug works well under the sofa. Bedrooms typically benefit from 5x8 or 6x9 sizes. Measure your space before purchasing.

How do I clean and maintain a shag area rug?

Vacuum weekly with a high-pile setting to prevent matting. Rotate the rug monthly for even wear. Spot-clean spills immediately with a damp cloth, and consider professional cleaning annually.

Are shag rugs good for high-traffic areas?

Shag rugs work best in low to moderate traffic areas like bedrooms and reading nooks. They can mat down in high-traffic zones, so consider a lower pile rug for entryways or living rooms with heavy foot traffic.

What colors work best with shag area rugs?

Neutral shades like gray, beige, and white are versatile and hide dirt well. Darker colors like charcoal and navy add sophistication, while soft pastels complement modern and eclectic decor styles.
