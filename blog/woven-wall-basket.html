Frequently Asked Questions

How do I hang a woven wall basket securely?

Use wall anchors or studs appropriate for your wall type. For drywall, toggle bolts or heavy-duty anchors work best. Always follow the manufacturer's weight recommendations and use a level for proper alignment.

What sizes of woven wall baskets are available?

Woven wall baskets range from small 8-inch accent pieces to large 24+ inch statement baskets. Consider your wall space and décor balance when selecting size.

Can woven baskets be used in kitchens and bathrooms?

Yes, many woven baskets work in humid environments, though natural materials may absorb moisture. Choose moisture-resistant weaves or sealed baskets for bathrooms and kitchens.

How do I clean and maintain woven wall baskets?

Dust regularly with a soft cloth or vacuum attachment. For deeper cleaning, use a dry brush. Avoid excess moisture and direct sunlight to prevent fading and material degradation.
