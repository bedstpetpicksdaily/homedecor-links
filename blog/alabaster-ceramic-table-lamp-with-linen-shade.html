Frequently Asked Questions

How stable is this lamp really? Will it tip if bumped?

The ceramic base is substantial enough that casual bumps won't topple it—we tested this during furniture rearrangement and it stayed put. You'd need a legitimate knock or a small child actively pulling on the shade to cause issues. That said, it's not indestructible if knocked hard against furniture, so it's not ideal for chaotic toddler bedrooms, but it's far more stable than thin-based designer lamps.

Does the linen shade get dingy quickly?

Not as quickly as polyester shades. The linen resists dust settling on the surface, though after several months in a dusty room it will pick up some discoloration. A gentle vacuum with a soft brush attachment keeps it looking fresh. It's also forgiving—minor marks don't show like they do on white synthetic shades.

What's the actual cord length, and can I use an extension cord?

Most versions come with a 5-6 foot cord, which works for bedside tables directly next to outlets but often falls short for living room placement. Yes, you can use an extension cord—many reviewers do—but it does create visual clutter if you don't have a way to hide it. Plan your placement before ordering, or budget for a quality extension cord you're willing to keep visible.
