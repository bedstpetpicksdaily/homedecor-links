Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes do metal wall sculptures typically come in?

Metal wall sculptures range from small 12-inch pieces to large 48+ inch installations. Consider your wall space and room size when selecting dimensions for optimal visual impact.

How do I install metal wall hanging sculptures?

Most metal sculptures include mounting hardware and instructions. Locate wall studs when possible for secure installation, or use appropriate wall anchors for drywall. Ensure the wall can support the weight of heavier pieces.

Are metal wall sculptures suitable for outdoor use?

Many metal wall sculptures are designed for both indoor and outdoor use. Verify product specifications and choose weather-resistant finishes like powder-coated or stainless steel for exterior applications.

How should I care for and maintain metal wall decor?

Dust metal sculptures regularly with a soft cloth. For outdoor pieces, periodically inspect for rust or weathering. Apply protective coatings as needed to maintain appearance and longevity.
