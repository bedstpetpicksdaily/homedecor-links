Frequently Asked Questions

Do blackout curtains really save energy?

Yes, blackout curtains with thermal insulation can reduce heat loss in winter and block heat gain in summer, potentially lowering your heating and cooling costs by 10-25% depending on your climate and usage.

Can blackout curtains help with noise reduction?

Blackout curtains provide some sound dampening due to their thick, dense fabric. While they won't completely soundproof a room, they can significantly reduce outside noise and echo.

What size blackout curtains do I need?

Measure your window width and add 8-12 inches for proper coverage. For length, measure from the top of your window to the floor or sill depending on your preferred style and mount height.

Are blackout curtains difficult to clean?

Most blackout curtains can be machine washed on a gentle cycle or vacuumed regularly. Check the manufacturer's care instructions, as some may require dry cleaning to maintain their thermal properties.
