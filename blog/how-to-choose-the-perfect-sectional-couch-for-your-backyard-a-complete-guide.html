Understanding Your Space and Layout

Before shopping for a sectional couch, measure your backyard carefully. Consider the available space, foot traffic patterns, and how the furniture will complement existing features like patios, decks, or gazebos. A sectional works best in open areas where guests can move around comfortably. Think about whether you want an L-shaped, U-shaped, or modular design. Frequently Asked Questions

What size sectional couch should I buy for my backyard?

Measure your backyard space and leave at least 2-3 feet of clearance on all sides for walking. A typical sectional ranges from 6-12 feet in length. AECOJOY offers various sizes and modular configurations, so you can choose or customize a sectional that perfectly fits your available space.

How do I protect my outdoor sectional from weather damage?

Use high-quality weatherproof covers when not in use, especially during winter or rainy seasons. Store cushions in an outdoor storage shed when the furniture isn't being used. AECOJOY sectionals are made with weather-resistant materials, but proper care extends their lifespan significantly.

Can I leave my sectional outside year-round?

This depends on your climate. In mild climates, you can leave it out year-round with proper covers. In harsh winters or rainy seasons, it's best to store cushions indoors or in a protective storage solution. AECOJOY's durable frames can withstand outdoor conditions, but cushions benefit from seasonal storage.

What's the best material for an outdoor sectional couch?

Look for solution-dyed fabrics, high-quality outdoor upholstery, and rust-resistant frames. Polyester and acrylic blends offer excellent durability and fade resistance. AECOJOY uses premium materials that resist mildew, UV fading, and moisture while remaining soft and comfortable.

How do I clean and maintain my outdoor sectional?

Regularly brush off debris, wipe with mild soap and water, and allow to air dry. Avoid harsh chemicals that can damage fabrics. For deep cleaning, use a soft brush and outdoor furniture cleaner. AECOJOY provides care instructions with their sectionals to maximize longevity.
