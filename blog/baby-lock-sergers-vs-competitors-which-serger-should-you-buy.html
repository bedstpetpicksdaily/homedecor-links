Frequently Asked Questions

Is a Baby Lock serger worth the investment compared to cheaper brands?

Yes. Baby Lock sergers offer superior threading systems, better build quality, and excellent customer support that justify the slightly higher investment. You'll spend less time troubleshooting and more time sewing, making the value exceptional over the machine's lifetime.

What's the difference between 4-thread and 5-thread sergers?

A 4-thread serger creates basic overlock stitches for sealing edges, while 5-thread sergers add a chain stitch for stability. Baby Lock's 5-thread models offer more versatility, especially for garment construction and decorative applications.

Are Baby Lock sergers difficult to thread?

No. Baby Lock sergers feature color-coded threading guides and simplified systems that make threading straightforward, even for beginners. Most users can thread a Baby Lock serger in minutes, compared to 15-30 minutes on competitor models.
