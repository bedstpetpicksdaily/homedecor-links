Frequently Asked Questions

What size is best for a living room?

A 5x7 geometric boho area rug is ideal for most living rooms, anchoring furniture arrangements while leaving border space visible around room edges.

How do I clean a boho area rug?

Vacuum regularly with a brush attachment, spot-clean spills immediately with mild soap and water, and professionally clean annually for deep maintenance.

Are geometric boho rugs durable?

Yes, quality boho rugs with polypropylene or wool fibers are highly durable and handle high-traffic areas well when properly maintained.

What decor styles pair with geometric boho rugs?

These rugs complement bohemian, eclectic, southwestern, and modern farmhouse styles, plus contemporary spaces seeking artistic accent pieces.
