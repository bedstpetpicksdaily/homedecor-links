Buying Guide: 3 Things to Look For

  • Material: Choose between wool for luxury and durability, synthetic blends for affordability, or natural fibers like jute for eco-friendly options. Consider your lifestyle and maintenance preferences when selecting materials.
  • Size & Placement: Measure your space carefully and consider how the rug will interact with your furniture. A general rule is to have at least the front legs of your seating area on the rug for visual cohesion.
  • Stain Resistance: If you have kids or pets, look for rugs with stain-resistant treatments or darker patterns that hide wear and tear. Many bestselling rugs now include built-in protection against spills and dirt.

FAQ: Common Questions About Area Rugs

Q: How often should I clean my area rug?

A: Vacuum high-traffic areas 2-3 times per week and do a deep clean every 12-18 months. Spot clean spills immediately to prevent permanent staining. Regular maintenance extends the life of your rug significantly.

Q: What size area rug should I buy?

A: Common sizes are 5x8 for bedrooms and smaller spaces, 8x10 for living rooms, and 9x12 for large open areas. Measure your room and consider your furniture layout to ensure proper proportion and balance.
