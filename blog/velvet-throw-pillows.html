Frequently Asked Questions

How do I clean velvet throw pillows?

Gently vacuum with an upholstery brush attachment or use a soft lint roller. For stains, blot with a clean, damp cloth and mild soap. Always test on a hidden area first and avoid saturating the fabric.

What sizes do velvet throw pillows come in?

Most velvet throw pillows range from 12x12 inches to 20x20 inches. Many retailers also offer lumbar pillow sizes (12x20 inches) and larger accent pillows for maximum decorative impact.

Are velvet throw pillows suitable for outdoor use?

Traditional velvet is best for indoor use only. However, outdoor velvet-like fabrics are available that resist moisture and fading, making them suitable for covered outdoor spaces.

How many velvet throw pillows should I use on my sofa?

For a standard sofa, 2-4 throw pillows create a balanced, styled look. Mix sizes and textures—pair larger pillows with smaller accent pillows for visual interest and dimension.
