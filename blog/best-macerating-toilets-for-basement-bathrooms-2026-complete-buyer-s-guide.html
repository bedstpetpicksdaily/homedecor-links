What Are Macerating Toilets and How Do They Work?

Macerating toilets are specialized fixtures equipped with a grinding mechanism that breaks down human waste and toilet paper into a liquefied form. This processed waste is then pumped through small-diameter pipes upward to your home's main sewer line or septic system, making them ideal for basements where traditional gravity-fed plumbing isn't feasible. Simple Project's macerating toilet systems feature advanced grinding technology that ensures efficient processing while maintaining quiet operation, a critical consideration for basement installations where noise can travel throughout your home.
