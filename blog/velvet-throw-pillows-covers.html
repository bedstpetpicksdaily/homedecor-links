Frequently Asked Questions

What size velvet pillow covers are most popular?

The 18x18 inch size is the most versatile and popular choice for sofas and accent chairs. However, 16x16, 20x20, and 22x22 options are also widely available depending on your furniture dimensions.

Are velvet throw pillow covers machine washable?

Most quality velvet covers are machine washable on gentle cycles with cold water. Always check the care instructions on individual products and consider using a laundry bag to protect the delicate fabric.

How do I prevent velvet pillow covers from getting damaged?

To protect velvet covers, avoid placing them in direct sunlight, use them away from high-traffic areas when possible, and brush gently with a soft cloth to maintain the pile. Regular vacuuming with an upholstery attachment helps remove dust.

What colors of velvet pillow covers are easiest to maintain?

Lighter colors like cream, gray, and blush show less dust but may stain more easily. Darker jewel tones like navy, emerald, and burgundy hide dust better but require regular gentle cleaning to maintain their appearance.
