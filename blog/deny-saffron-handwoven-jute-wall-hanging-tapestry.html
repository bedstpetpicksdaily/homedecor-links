Frequently Asked Questions

What are the dimensions of the Deny & Saffron jute wall tapestry?

While specific dimensions vary by listing, most Deny & Saffron jute wall hangings range from 40x60 inches to 60x80 inches. Check the Amazon product page for exact measurements of the specific version you're interested in.

How do I clean and maintain this jute wall tapestry?

Gently vacuum with an upholstery attachment monthly to remove dust from the woven texture. Avoid direct water exposure and harsh chemicals. For spot cleaning, use a soft, dry cloth. Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent fading.

Is this tapestry suitable for high-humidity rooms like bathrooms?

Natural jute fiber is sensitive to excess moisture. While it's acceptable for regular bedrooms and living rooms, we don't recommend installing it in bathrooms, kitchens, or laundry rooms where humidity levels are consistently high.

Can I hang this wall tapestry on any wall surface?

Yes, the lightweight jute tapestry works on drywall, plaster, and painted surfaces. Use appropriate wall anchors for your wall type, or opt for a tension rod hanger for a damage-free installation option.

What boho decor items pair well with this jute tapestry?

Complement this tapestry with macramé wall hangings, woven throw pillows, natural fiber rugs, rattan accents, plants, and organic linen curtains for a cohesive bohemian aesthetic.
