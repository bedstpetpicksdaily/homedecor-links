Frequently Asked Questions

What size geometric area rug should I choose?

Measure your room and furniture layout. For living rooms, choose a rug large enough to anchor seating areas (typically 5x8 or 8x10). Bedrooms work well with 5x7 rugs, while entryways suit smaller 2x3 or 3x5 options.

How do I clean and maintain a geometric area rug?

Vacuum regularly to remove dust and debris. For stains, use a damp cloth with mild soap and water. Synthetic fiber rugs are generally easier to clean than natural materials like wool or jute.

Do geometric rugs work with all décor styles?

Yes! Modern geometric patterns complement contemporary, minimalist, and industrial styles. Softer geometric designs work well with bohemian and eclectic décor when paired with complementary throw pillows and curtains.

Are geometric area rugs pet-friendly?

Many geometric rugs are pet-friendly, especially those made from synthetic fibers or low-pile wool. These materials resist staining and are easier to clean when accidents occur.
