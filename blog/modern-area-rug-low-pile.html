Frequently Asked Questions

What is low pile carpet and why should I choose it?

Low pile carpet features short, tightly woven fibers (typically under 1/4 inch) that are easy to clean and maintain. It's perfect for high-traffic areas and homes with pets or children due to its durability and stain resistance.

How do I clean and maintain a modern low pile area rug?

Vacuum your low pile rug weekly using a regular vacuum cleaner, and address spills immediately with a damp cloth. For deeper cleaning, use a carpet cleaner or hire professional services annually to maintain its appearance.

What size area rug should I choose for my living room?

Measure your space and choose a rug that anchors your furniture arrangement. For most living rooms, a 5x8 or 8x10 rug works well, but ensure at least the front legs of your sofa sit on the rug for visual balance.

Are low pile rugs suitable for all interior design styles?

Yes, modern low pile rugs work with contemporary, industrial, transitional, and even traditional styles. Available in countless patterns and colors, you can find designs that perfectly match your home's aesthetic.

How long do quality low pile area rugs typically last?

With proper care and maintenance, a quality modern low pile rug can last 5-10 years or longer. Durability depends on material quality, foot traffic levels, and how well you maintain the rug.
