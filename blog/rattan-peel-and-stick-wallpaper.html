Frequently Asked Questions

Can rattan peel and stick wallpaper be installed in bathrooms?

While possible, it's not recommended for high-moisture areas. The humidity can weaken the adhesive and damage the natural rattan texture. Consider moisture-resistant alternatives for bathrooms.

How long does rattan peel and stick wallpaper last?

Quality rattan wallpaper typically lasts 3-5 years in normal conditions. Longevity depends on wall preparation, humidity levels, and proper installation.

Will rattan wallpaper damage my walls when removed?

No, premium peel and stick wallpaper is designed to remove cleanly without leaving residue or damaging paint, making it perfect for rental properties.

What room styles work best with rattan wallpaper?

Rattan pairs beautifully with coastal, bohemian, farmhouse, and modern minimalist styles. It complements natural wood furniture, woven accents, and neutral color palettes perfectly.
