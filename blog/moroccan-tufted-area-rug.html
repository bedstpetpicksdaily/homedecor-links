Frequently Asked Questions

What size Moroccan tufted area rug should I buy?

Choose based on your room layout: 5x7 or 5x8 for living rooms, 8x10 for larger spaces, and 3x5 for bedrooms or accent areas. Measure your furniture arrangement to ensure proper placement.

How do I clean and maintain a Moroccan tufted rug?

Vacuum regularly with a gentle brush, spot-clean spills immediately, and have professionally cleaned annually. Avoid excessive moisture and direct sunlight to preserve colors and texture.

What décor styles pair well with Moroccan tufted rugs?

These rugs complement bohemian, eclectic, Mediterranean, and modern décor. Pair with decorative throw pillows, wall art, lamps, and curtains in complementary earth tones or jewel colors.

Are Moroccan tufted rugs durable for high-traffic areas?

Yes, authentic tufted construction is highly durable. They're suitable for living rooms and hallways but may benefit from protective pads underneath for longevity.
