Frequently Asked Questions

What room is best for an arc floor lamp?

Arc floor lamps work beautifully in living rooms, bedrooms, and over seating areas. They're perfect for creating ambient lighting near sofas, chairs, or reading nooks without requiring additional side tables.

How do I ensure my arc lamp won't tip over?

Look for lamps with weighted, sturdy bases. Arc floor lamps work best with heavier base materials like metal or marble to maintain stability and safety in your home.

Can arc floor lamps work with modern decor?

Yes! Metal arc lamps in black, brass, or chrome finishes pair perfectly with modern, minimalist, and industrial interior design styles.

What bulb type should I use?

Most modern arc lamps accommodate LED or incandescent bulbs. LED options are energy-efficient and provide long-lasting, bright illumination for your decorative accent lighting.
