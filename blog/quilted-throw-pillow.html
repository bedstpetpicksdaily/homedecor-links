Frequently Asked Questions

What size quilted throw pillow should I choose?

The best size depends on your furniture and space. Square pillows typically range from 16" to 24". For sofas, 20" pillows are versatile, while smaller 16" pillows work well for chairs and accent spaces.

How do I care for quilted throw pillows?

Most quilted throw pillows can be spot-cleaned with mild soap and water. Fluff them regularly to maintain shape, and check the care label for specific washing instructions, as some may be machine washable while others require dry cleaning.

Can quilted throw pillows work with any decor style?

Yes! Quilted throw pillows are incredibly versatile and complement various design styles including modern, farmhouse, traditional, bohemian, and eclectic decor. Choose colors and patterns that coordinate with your existing palette.

What materials are best for quilted throw pillows?

Popular materials include cotton, linen, velvet, and polyester blends. Cotton and linen offer breathability, while velvet provides luxury and richness. Choose based on your desired look, feel, and maintenance preferences.
