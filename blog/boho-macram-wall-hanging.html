Frequently Asked Questions

What size macramé wall hanging should I choose for my space?

Measure your wall space and choose a macramé hanging that covers 25-40% of the wall area. Larger rooms work well with bigger pieces (48+ inches), while smaller spaces benefit from medium-sized hangings (24-36 inches).

How do I clean and maintain my boho macramé wall hanging?

Gently dust with a soft brush or cloth monthly. For deeper cleaning, use a handheld vacuum with upholstery attachment on low setting. Avoid water and harsh chemicals that may damage natural fibers.

Can macramé wall hangings work with modern decor styles?

Absolutely! Macramé pieces bridge bohemian and contemporary design. Pair them with minimalist furniture, neutral walls, and modern accents for a balanced eclectic aesthetic.

What materials are best for macramé wall hangings?

Cotton, jute, and wool are popular choices. Cotton offers softness and durability, jute provides natural texture, while wool adds warmth. Choose based on your decor style and maintenance preferences.
