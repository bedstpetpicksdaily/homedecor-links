Frequently Asked Questions

Are Edison bulb table lamps energy efficient?

Modern LED Edison bulbs are highly energy efficient, using 80% less electricity than traditional incandescent versions while providing the same warm, vintage aesthetic.

What room styles work best with Edison bulb table lamps?

Edison bulb lamps complement industrial, rustic, vintage, bohemian, and contemporary styles. Their versatile design makes them suitable for living rooms, bedrooms, home offices, and entryways.

How long do Edison bulbs last?

Traditional filament Edison bulbs last 1,000-3,000 hours, while LED Edison bulbs can last 15,000-25,000 hours, making them a more durable long-term investment.

Can I use Edison bulb lamps in any lamp fixture?

Most Edison bulbs use standard E26 or E27 sockets, making them compatible with most traditional lamp bases. Always check your lamp's socket type before purchasing.
