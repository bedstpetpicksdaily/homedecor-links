Frequently Asked Questions

Are jute area rugs safe for pets and children?

Yes, jute rugs are generally safe for families and pets since they're made from natural plant fibers without harsh chemicals. However, the texture can be rough on sensitive skin, so consider pairing with soft throw pillows or blankets for extra comfort.

How do you clean a woven jute area rug?

Vacuum regularly with a soft brush attachment to remove dust and fibers. For spills, blot immediately with a dry cloth. Avoid wet cleaning methods, as moisture can damage jute. For deep cleaning, use a dry cleaning service experienced with natural fiber rugs.

What size jute rug should I choose for my room?

For living rooms, choose a rug large enough to anchor your furniture (typically 8x10 feet). For bedrooms, a smaller 5x8 feet rug works well. Measure your space and consider furniture placement to determine the ideal size.

Can jute area rugs be used in kitchens or bathrooms?

Jute is moisture-sensitive and not recommended for kitchens or bathrooms. These high-moisture areas can cause mold and mildew. Reserve jute rugs for dry spaces like bedrooms, living rooms, and home offices.
