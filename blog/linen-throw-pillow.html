Frequently Asked Questions

Are linen throw pillows good for allergies?

Yes, linen is hypoallergenic and naturally resistant to dust mites, making it an excellent choice for allergy sufferers seeking comfortable home décor solutions.

How do I care for linen throw pillows?

Most linen pillows can be machine washed in cool water with mild detergent. Air dry or use low heat. Some wrinkling is normal and adds to linen's natural aesthetic appeal.

What sizes do linen throw pillows come in?

Linen throw pillows are available in various sizes, typically ranging from 12x12 inches to 20x20 inches, with some specialty sizes available for unique spaces.

Do linen pillows fade easily?

Quality linen pillows are dyed with colorfast dyes that resist fading. Keep pillows away from direct sunlight to maintain vibrant colors longer.
