Frequently Asked Questions

What size Moroccan rug is best for a living room?

A 5x7 or 8x10 geometric Moroccan rug works well for most living rooms, providing adequate coverage under furniture while defining the seating area. Measure your space before purchasing to ensure proper fit.

Are geometric Moroccan rugs easy to clean?

Yes, most machine-made Moroccan rugs are easy to maintain. Vacuum regularly, spot-clean spills immediately with mild soap and water, and avoid excessive moisture. Check care instructions for your specific rug.

Do Moroccan rugs work in modern decor?

Absolutely. Geometric patterns in Moroccan rugs complement both bohemian and contemporary design styles, serving as statement pieces or subtle accents depending on color and pattern intensity.

How long do Moroccan area rugs typically last?

Quality geometric Moroccan rugs last 7-10 years or longer with proper care. Durability depends on material quality, foot traffic, and maintenance practices.
