Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes of metal wall art are available?

Metal wall art comes in various sizes ranging from small 12-inch pieces for accent walls to large 48-inch installations that serve as statement pieces. Measure your wall space and consider furniture placement when selecting the appropriate size.

How do I hang metal wall art securely?

Use heavy-duty wall anchors or studs for secure installation. For drywall, toggle bolts or molly bolts work best. Consult the product instructions for weight requirements and ensure you use appropriate hardware.

Can metal wall art work with different decor styles?

Yes, metal wall art is incredibly versatile and complements modern, industrial, rustic, bohemian, and transitional styles. Pair it with matching rugs, throw pillows, curtains, and lamps to create a cohesive look.

Is metal wall art easy to clean and maintain?

Metal wall art requires minimal maintenance. Simply dust regularly with a soft cloth and occasionally wipe with a damp cloth. Most pieces are treated to resist rust and fading, ensuring lasting beauty.
