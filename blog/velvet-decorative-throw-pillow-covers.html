Frequently Asked Questions

What size velvet throw pillow covers are most popular?

The 18x18 inch size is the most popular choice for standard throw pillows, fitting perfectly on couches, chairs, and beds. However, 20x20, 16x16, and lumbar sizes are also widely available to suit different furniture arrangements.

How do I clean velvet pillow covers?

Most velvet covers should be gently brushed with a soft-bristled brush to remove dust. For stains, use a damp cloth with mild soap and water, or follow the manufacturer's care instructions. Dry cleaning is recommended for delicate or valuable pieces.

Are velvet pillow covers suitable for all room styles?

Yes, velvet covers work beautifully in modern, traditional, transitional, and eclectic interiors. Choose colors and patterns that complement your existing decor, wall art, rugs, curtains, and other decorative accents for a cohesive look.

Can velvet pillow covers fade over time?

High-quality velvet covers are colorfast and resist fading when kept away from direct sunlight. Rotating your pillows periodically and using curtains or blinds to control sun exposure helps preserve color vibrancy.
