Frequently Asked Questions

How do I clean velvet throw pillow covers?

Most velvet covers can be gently vacuumed with a soft brush attachment or wiped with a dry cloth. For stains, use a lint roller or professional dry cleaning service. Always check manufacturer care instructions before cleaning.

What sizes do velvet pillow covers come in?

Velvet throw pillow covers typically come in 18x18 inches, 20x20 inches, and 22x22 inches sizes. Some sets offer multiple sizes, allowing you to mix and match for layered pillow arrangements.

Are velvet pillow covers suitable for outdoor use?

Standard velvet covers are best suited for indoor use as they can fade in sunlight and may be damaged by moisture. Look for outdoor velvet alternatives if you need patio décor solutions.

Can I use velvet pillow covers on any pillow insert?

Yes, most velvet throw pillow covers feature zipper closures and fit standard pillow inserts. Ensure you match the cover size to your pillow insert for proper fit and appearance.
