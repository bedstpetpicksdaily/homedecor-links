Frequently Asked Questions

What size area rug should I choose for my living room?

Measure your living room and consider furniture placement. A standard living room rug is typically 8x10 feet, which anchors seating areas. Ensure the rug extends at least 18 inches beyond your sofa on all sides for proper proportions.

Are modern geometric rugs easy to clean?

Yes, most modern area rugs made from synthetic fibers are easy to clean with regular vacuuming. For spills, blot immediately with a damp cloth. Professional cleaning is recommended annually for deeper maintenance.

What materials are best for modern area rugs?

Popular options include polypropylene (durable and stain-resistant), wool (natural and luxurious), and cotton blends (soft and affordable). Choose based on your lifestyle, budget, and desired aesthetic.

How can I prevent my area rug from slipping?

Use a non-slip rug pad underneath to keep your modern area rug in place and protect your floors. This also adds cushioning and extends the rug's lifespan.
