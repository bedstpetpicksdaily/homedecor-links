FAQ: Common Questions About Decorative Vases

Can I use decorative vases for fresh flowers?

Yes! Most decorative vases can hold fresh flowers, though you'll want to ensure they're waterproof or can be lined with a waterproof material. Glass and ceramic vases work particularly well for fresh arrangements, while some decorative-only pieces may have sealed bottoms.

What's the best way to style decorative vases?

Group vases in odd numbers (3 or 5) on shelves or tables for visual interest, vary their heights and sizes, and fill them with dried flowers, branches, pampas grass, or leave them empty for a minimalist look. Consider your color scheme and mix complementary styles for an curated, designer appearance.
