Frequently Asked Questions

Can all machine washable rugs go in a standard washing machine?

Most machine washable area rugs are designed for standard washing machines, but always check manufacturer instructions first. Some larger rugs may require commercial machines at laundromats.

How often should I wash my machine washable area rug?

High-traffic areas benefit from monthly washing, while moderate-use rugs can be washed quarterly. Spot clean spills immediately and vacuum regularly between washes.

Will machine washing damage the rug's color or texture?

Quality machine washable rugs use colorfastness technology and durable synthetic fibers designed to maintain appearance after repeated washing when proper care instructions are followed.

What size machine washable rug should I buy for my living room?

Measure your space first. Popular living room sizes range from 5x7 to 8x10 feet. Ensure the rug anchors furniture groupings while leaving border space around edges.
