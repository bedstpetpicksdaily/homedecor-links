Frequently Asked Questions

What size blackout curtain panels do I need?

Measure your window width and height, then select panels that are 1.5 to 2 times your window width for proper fullness and coverage. Most panels come in standard lengths of 63, 84, 95, or 108 inches.

Are blackout curtains good for energy efficiency?

Yes, thermal insulated blackout curtains significantly reduce energy costs by preventing heat transfer through windows, helping maintain consistent indoor temperatures year-round.

Can I machine wash blackout curtain panels?

Most blackout curtains are machine washable on gentle cycle with cold water. Check the manufacturer's care instructions before washing to ensure proper maintenance.

Do rod pocket curtains work with all curtain rods?

Rod pocket curtains work best with standard straight rods. They may not be compatible with curved, corner, or specialty rods designed for other hanging systems.
