Frequently Asked Questions

What does 5x8 mean for an area rug?

A 5x8 area rug measures 5 feet wide by 8 feet long, making it ideal for living rooms, bedrooms, and dining areas. This size provides substantial floor coverage without overwhelming smaller spaces.

Are Persian style rugs suitable for modern homes?

Yes, Persian style rugs work beautifully in modern homes. Their intricate patterns and rich colors can add warmth and sophistication to contemporary interiors when paired with minimalist furniture and neutral wall art.

How do I clean and maintain a Persian area rug?

Vacuum regularly with a gentle brush, rotate the rug every few months, and have it professionally cleaned annually. Avoid direct sunlight and place a rug pad underneath to prevent slipping and damage.

What's the difference between authentic and machine-made Persian rugs?

Authentic hand-knotted Persian rugs are crafted by artisans and are more durable and valuable, while machine-made versions are more affordable, easier to maintain, and offer excellent style at a budget-friendly price point.
