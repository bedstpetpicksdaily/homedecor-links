Frequently Asked Questions

How do I care for a faux fur throw blanket?

Most faux fur throws can be gently machine washed on a delicate cycle in cold water or hand washed. Air dry completely to maintain the soft texture, and avoid high heat to prevent damage to the fibers.

What sizes do faux fur throw blankets come in?

Faux fur throws typically range from small accent throws (40x50 inches) to larger blankets (60x80 inches). Choose based on your furniture size and intended use.

Are faux fur throw blankets suitable for all seasons?

While faux fur throws provide excellent warmth, they're most popular in fall and winter. Lighter weight options work well year-round for adding texture and style to your home decor.

What colors of faux fur throws are most popular?

Neutral tones like white, gray, black, and beige are timeless choices that complement any decor. Trendy colors include blush, navy, and brown for a modern touch to your interior design.
