Frequently Asked Questions

Can brushed gold wall sconces work with different interior design styles?

Yes, brushed gold sconces are highly versatile. They complement modern, contemporary, industrial, transitional, and even mid-century modern design styles, making them an adaptable choice for any home decor theme.

Are brushed gold wall sconces easy to install?

Most brushed gold wall sconces come with mounting hardware and instructions. While basic installation is straightforward, it's recommended to hire a licensed electrician for safe wiring installation to ensure proper electrical connections.

What's the difference between brushed gold and polished gold finishes?

Brushed gold has a matte, subtle finish that's more contemporary and hides fingerprints better, while polished gold is shiny and reflective. Brushed gold is generally preferred for modern home decor as it's less prone to showing marks and dust.

Do these sconces work with dimmers?

Many brushed gold wall sconces are dimmable when paired with LED bulbs and compatible dimmer switches. Always check product specifications before purchasing to ensure dimmer compatibility.
