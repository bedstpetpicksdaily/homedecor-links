Frequently Asked Questions

Are LED floor lamps with dimmers compatible with all switches?

Not all LED lamps work with older dimmer switches. Look for lamps labeled as dimmable and check compatibility with your specific dimmer model before purchasing.

How much can I save with an LED floor lamp compared to traditional lamps?

LED floor lamps use 75-80% less energy than incandescent bulbs, potentially saving $50-100 annually per lamp depending on usage.

What's the lifespan of LED bulbs in floor lamps?

Quality LED bulbs last 25,000-50,000 hours, which translates to 10-20 years of typical use, significantly longer than traditional bulbs.

Can LED floor lamps work as the main light source in a room?

Yes, many modern LED floor lamps provide sufficient brightness to serve as primary lighting, especially those with higher wattage ratings and quality lenses.
