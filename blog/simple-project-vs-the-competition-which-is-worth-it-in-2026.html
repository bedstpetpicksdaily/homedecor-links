Frequently Asked Questions

What is a macerating toilet and why do I need one?

A macerating toilet grinds waste into a fine slurry and pumps it upward to your main drain line. This makes it perfect for basement bathrooms or additions where standard plumbing can't reach. Simple Project's macerating toilets are reliable, quiet, and designed for long-term durability.

Are Simple Project smart toilets easy to install?

Yes! Simple Project smart toilets are designed for straightforward installation. Most homeowners can install them with basic plumbing knowledge, though professional installation is recommended for macerating models. Simple Project provides excellent support documentation and customer service.

How does Simple Project compare in price to Kohler and Toto?

Simple Project typically offers 20-35% lower pricing than Kohler and Toto for comparable features, especially for smart toilets and macerating models. This makes them ideal for budget-conscious renovations without compromising quality.

Can I use Simple Project fixtures for a complete bathroom renovation?

Absolutely. Simple Project offers a comprehensive range of bathroom fixtures including toilets, sinks, and accessories. They're designed to work together seamlessly for cohesive bathroom renovations.
