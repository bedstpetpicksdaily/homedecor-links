Frequently Asked Questions

What size room is best for a 5x7 Persian area rug?

A 5x7 rug works perfectly in bedrooms, living rooms under 300 sq ft, dining areas, and home offices. It's large enough to anchor furniture while fitting comfortably in most residential spaces without overwhelming the room.

How do I clean and maintain a Persian style area rug?

Vacuum regularly with a non-beater brush, rotate quarterly to prevent uneven wear, and spot-clean spills immediately. Professional cleaning every 2-3 years helps preserve colors and extend the rug's lifespan significantly.

Is a non-slip backing necessary for Persian rugs?

Non-slip backing prevents the rug from shifting on hardwood or tile floors, creating a safer environment and protecting your flooring. It's especially important in high-traffic areas and homes with children or pets.

What's the difference between authentic and machine-made Persian rugs?

Authentic hand-knotted Persian rugs are labor-intensive and more expensive but offer superior durability and unique character. Machine-made versions provide similar aesthetic appeal at lower prices with easier maintenance, making them ideal for budget-conscious shoppers.
