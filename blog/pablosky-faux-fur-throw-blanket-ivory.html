Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Pablosky faux fur throw blanket machine washable?

Most Pablosky faux fur throws are machine washable on gentle cycles with cold water. Always check the specific care label for detailed washing instructions to maintain the blanket's softness and appearance.

Will the ivory color fade or yellow over time?

Quality faux fur typically resists fading when cared for properly. Avoid prolonged direct sunlight exposure and use mild detergents to preserve the blanket's bright ivory tone and prevent discoloration.

What size should I choose for my furniture?

For sofas, a larger throw (around 60x80 inches) provides optimal coverage. For armchairs or beds, measure your furniture dimensions and choose accordingly. Larger throws also work beautifully as decorative wall accents.

How does faux fur compare to other throw materials?

Faux fur offers superior softness and luxury feel compared to fleece or cotton throws. It drapes elegantly, retains warmth effectively, and provides a high-end aesthetic that enhances home décor instantly.
