Frequently Asked Questions

What size room is best for a 5x7 area rug?

A 5x7 rug works perfectly in living rooms, master bedrooms, and dining areas. It typically anchors furniture groupings while leaving 12-18 inches of floor space around edges.

Are geometric modern rugs durable?

Yes, most modern geometric rugs use durable synthetic blends or wool that resist staining and wear. Quality matters—invest in reputable brands for longevity.

How do I clean a modern area rug?

Vacuum regularly and spot-clean spills immediately with mild soap and water. Professional cleaning annually keeps your rug looking fresh and extends its lifespan.

What colors work best with modern rugs?

Grays, blacks, whites, and earth tones complement geometric modern designs. These neutral bases pair beautifully with throw pillows and decorative accents.
