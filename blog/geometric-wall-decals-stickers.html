Frequently Asked Questions

Are geometric wall decals removable?

Yes, most quality geometric wall decals are removable and repositionable. Look for stickers specifically labeled as removable to avoid wall damage when taking them down.

What surfaces work best for wall decals?

Wall decals adhere best to smooth, clean, dry surfaces like painted drywall. They may not stick properly to textured walls, wallpaper, or unprimed surfaces.

How long do geometric wall decals last?

Quality removable decals typically last 2-5 years, depending on wall conditions, humidity, and sun exposure. Higher-end options offer greater durability.

Can I use wall decals in rental properties?

Absolutely! Removable geometric wall decals are ideal for renters since they don't damage walls and can be easily removed when moving.
