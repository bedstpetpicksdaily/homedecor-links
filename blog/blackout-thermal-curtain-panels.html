Frequently Asked Questions

Do blackout thermal curtains really save energy?

Yes, thermal curtains can reduce heat loss by up to 25% in winter and block heat gain in summer, potentially lowering your energy bills by 10-15% annually.

What size blackout curtains do I need?

Measure your window width and height. For best coverage and thermal efficiency, panels should extend 8-12 inches beyond the window frame on each side and hang from ceiling to floor.

How do I clean blackout thermal curtains?

Most blackout curtains can be gently vacuumed with a brush attachment or hand-washed in cool water. Always check manufacturer care instructions before washing.

Can blackout curtains work with my home decor?

Absolutely. Modern blackout thermal panels come in numerous colors, patterns, and textures that complement any interior design style from minimalist to traditional.
