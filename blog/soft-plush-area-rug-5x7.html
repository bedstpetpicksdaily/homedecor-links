Frequently Asked Questions

What size room is a 5x7 rug best for?

A 5x7 area rug works perfectly for medium-sized living rooms, master bedrooms, dining rooms, and home offices. It provides enough coverage to anchor furniture while fitting comfortably in most spaces.

How do I care for a plush area rug?

Vacuum regularly (2-3 times weekly), use a carpet protector spray, spot clean spills immediately, and have it professionally cleaned annually to maintain softness and prevent matting.

Can I use a plush rug in high-traffic areas?

While plush rugs are durable, they're best suited for moderate-traffic areas. High-traffic zones may cause faster matting and wear. Consider lower-pile alternatives for entryways and hallways.

What decor styles work with soft plush rugs?

Plush rugs complement virtually any design style—modern, traditional, bohemian, and contemporary. They pair beautifully with throw pillows, curtains, lamps, and wall art for cohesive home decor.
