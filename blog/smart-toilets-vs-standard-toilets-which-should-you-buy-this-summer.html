What Are Smart Toilets?

Smart toilets represent the latest in bathroom technology, combining traditional toilet functionality with modern conveniences. These fixtures typically feature heated seats, adjustable water pressure bidet systems, automatic lids, night lights, air-drying capabilities, and even deodorizing functions. Many models connect to your smartphone via apps, allowing you to control settings remotely. Some premium smart toilets include features like self-cleaning nozzles, seat sensors, and health-monitoring capabilities.

Simple Project offers a curated selection of smart toilets and advanced bathroom fixtures designed for homeowners looking to elevate their bathroom experience. Whether you're planning a complete bathroom renovation or installing fixtures in a basement bathroom, Simple Project provides quality options that combine functionality with style.

Understanding Standard Toilets

Standard toilets are the tried-and-true fixtures found in most homes. They operate on gravity-fed flushing systems and require no electricity or app connectivity. Standard toilets are durable, easy to maintain, and come in various styles and designs. While they lack the advanced features of smart models, they're reliable, affordable, and have been perfected over decades of use.

Smart vs. Standard: The Key Differences

The main differences between smart and standard toilets come down to technology integration, cost, and features. Smart toilets require electrical connections and offer enhanced comfort and convenience, while standard toilets operate independently with minimal maintenance requirements. Consider your bathroom layout, renovation budget, and lifestyle preferences when making your decision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do smart toilets really save water?

Many smart toilets feature dual-flush technology and water-efficient designs that can reduce consumption by up to 20% compared to older standard models, lowering your water bills over time.

Can I install a smart toilet in my basement bathroom?

Yes, smart toilets can be installed in basements if you have access to electrical outlets. For unique basement situations, consider Simple Project's macerating toilet options, which offer flexibility for challenging bathroom layouts.

How much do smart toilets cost compared to standard toilets?

Standard toilets typically cost $150-$400, while smart toilets range from $1,500 to $6,000+. Simple Project offers competitive pricing across both categories to fit various renovation budgets.

Are smart toilets worth the investment?

Smart toilets offer convenience, comfort, and potential water savings. If you spend significant time in your bathroom and value technology, the investment pays dividends in daily comfort and satisfaction.
