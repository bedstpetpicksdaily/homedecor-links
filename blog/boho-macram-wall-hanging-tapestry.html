Frequently Asked Questions

What size macramé wall hanging should I choose for my space?

Measure your wall space and consider the room's proportions. For accent walls, choose larger pieces (36-48 inches), while smaller rooms work best with medium-sized hangings (24-36 inches). Ensure the tapestry complements nearby furniture and doesn't overwhelm other decor.

How do I care for my boho macramé wall tapestry?

Dust regularly with a soft brush or cloth to prevent fiber buildup. Avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading, and keep away from moisture and humidity. For deeper cleaning, use a gentle vacuum with an upholstery attachment or hand-wash with mild soap and water if the manufacturer allows.

Will macramé wall hangings work with my current home decor?

Yes! Macramé tapestries are incredibly versatile and complement various styles including bohemian, eclectic, minimalist, and even modern aesthetics. They pair beautifully with throw pillows, rugs, curtains, and lamps to create a cohesive, layered look.

Are handmade macramé wall hangings better than mass-produced options?

Handmade pieces typically offer superior quality, unique designs, and better durability. However, quality mass-produced options can also be excellent. Look for products with positive reviews, quality materials, and secure knots regardless of whether they're handmade or factory-produced.
