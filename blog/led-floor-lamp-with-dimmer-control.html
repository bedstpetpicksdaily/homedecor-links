Frequently Asked Questions

Do all LED floor lamps work with dimmer switches?

Not all LED lamps are compatible with standard dimmer switches. Look for lamps specifically labeled as 'dimmable' or those with built-in dimmer controls to ensure compatibility.

How much can you save with an LED floor lamp?

LED floor lamps use 75% less energy than incandescent bulbs and last 25,000+ hours, saving you significantly on electricity and replacement costs over time.

What colors do LED floor lamps come in?

Modern LED floor lamps are available in numerous finishes including black, white, chrome, brass, and wood tones to match any home decor style.

Can LED floor lamps be used in any room?

Yes, LED floor lamps work in living rooms, bedrooms, offices, reading nooks, and entryways. Choose designs that complement your existing rugs, curtains, and decorative accents.
