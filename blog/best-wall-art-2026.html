Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best way to hang wall art?

The general rule is to hang artwork at eye level, with the center of the piece positioned about 57-60 inches from the floor. For multiple pieces, arrange them with equal spacing and ensure they're level before securing with appropriate wall anchors for your wall type.

How do I choose the right size wall art?

As a guideline, your wall art should cover about 50-75% of the wall space it occupies. For a single large piece above a sofa, aim for artwork that's roughly 2/3 the width of the furniture. For smaller walls or tight spaces, multiple smaller pieces grouped together often work better than one oversized piece.
