Frequently Asked Questions

Do Edison pendant lights work with all ceiling types?

Most Edison pendant fixtures are compatible with standard ceiling installations. However, check your specific fixture's specifications and consult installation guidelines to ensure proper mounting on your ceiling type.

What bulb types work best with Edison vintage pendant lights?

Classic incandescent Edison bulbs provide authentic vintage warmth, but LED Edison bulbs are energy-efficient alternatives that offer similar aesthetics with lower electricity consumption.

Can I use Edison pendant lights in bathrooms?

Yes, but ensure the fixture has appropriate moisture-resistant ratings and is installed away from direct water sources to maintain durability and safety.

How do I clean and maintain my Edison pendant fixture?

Dust regularly with a soft cloth when the light is off and cool. Avoid harsh chemicals; use mild soap and water if needed. Check electrical connections periodically for safety.
