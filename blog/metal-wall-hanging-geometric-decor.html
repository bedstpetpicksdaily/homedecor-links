Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install metal wall hanging geometric decor?

Most metal wall art comes with mounting hardware and instructions. Simply locate wall studs or use appropriate anchors, mark your desired height (typically 57-60 inches from floor to center), and secure the piece with provided hardware. Always use a level to ensure straight installation.

What sizes of geometric metal wall art are available?

Sizes typically range from 12 inches to 48+ inches wide, with options for vertical, horizontal, and square configurations. Consider your wall space and existing furniture when selecting size to ensure proper visual balance.

Can metal geometric wall decor work in any room?

Yes, metal geometric art works in bedrooms, living rooms, kitchens, offices, and entryways. Choose finishes like matte black, brushed copper, or gold to coordinate with your existing decor and lighting in each space.

How do I clean and maintain metal wall art?

Dust regularly with a soft cloth and occasionally wipe with a damp cloth if needed. Avoid abrasive cleaners that could scratch the finish. Most metal pieces are low-maintenance and resistant to tarnishing and rust.
