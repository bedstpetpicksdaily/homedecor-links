Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a real Persian rug or synthetic?

Safavieh's Vintage collection at this price point uses machine-made construction with synthetic fibers, not hand-knotted wool. This explains the affordable pricing but means durability differs significantly from authentic Persian rugs.

Will the charcoal color hide dirt and stains?

Darker tones like charcoal excel at concealing dust and light soiling between cleanings. However, the distressed pattern is equally important—the varied texture breaks up visible dirt better than solid-colored alternatives.

How does an 8x10 work in different room layouts?

This size anchors living room seating arrangements when centered under a coffee table with sofas positioned around it. In bedrooms, it provides substantial under-bed coverage. For dining rooms, it accommodates most table shapes, though ensure at least 24 inches extends beyond the table on all sides.

What's the catch with a $10 rug?

This appears to be either a pricing error, clearance inventory, or volume-discount pricing through the affiliate link. Typical retail for comparable Safavieh pieces runs $50-150+. Verify current pricing before purchase, as availability at this rate may be limited.

Should I use a rug pad underneath?

Yes, absolutely. A quality rug pad prevents slipping on hard floors and protects your flooring underneath while extending the rug's life by reducing friction damage. This is non-negotiable regardless of rug quality.
