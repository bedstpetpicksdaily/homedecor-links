Frequently Asked Questions

Are Edison bulb table lamps energy efficient?

Modern LED Edison bulbs are highly energy-efficient, using 80% less electricity than traditional incandescent versions while providing the same warm, vintage aesthetic.

What room styles work best with industrial Edison lamps?

These lamps complement industrial, farmhouse, steampunk, bohemian, and mid-century modern styles. They also work well in contemporary spaces as statement pieces.

Do Edison bulb lamps provide enough light for reading?

Edison lamps are primarily ambient lighting fixtures. For task lighting, pair them with additional lighting sources or position near a window for adequate brightness.

How do I choose the right size Edison table lamp?

Consider your table height and room size. Standard table lamps measure 24-30 inches tall and work best on nightstands and side tables in medium to large rooms.
