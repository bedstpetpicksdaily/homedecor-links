Frequently Asked Questions

What size ceramic vase set should I choose?

Choose based on your space and decor needs. Small sets (3-4 inches) work well on shelves, medium sets (6-8 inches) suit side tables, and large sets (10+ inches) make dramatic floor or mantel statements.

Can ceramic vases hold fresh flowers?

Yes, ceramic vases are excellent for fresh flowers. Simply add water and change it every few days. The smooth interior prevents stem damage and keeps flowers fresh longer.

How do I clean ceramic decorative vases?

Gently wipe with a soft, damp cloth or use a feather duster for delicate finishes. For vases with narrow openings, use a bottle brush with warm soapy water, then rinse thoroughly and air dry.

What decor styles match ceramic vase sets?

Ceramic vases complement modern, minimalist, farmhouse, contemporary, boho, and traditional styles. Choose neutral tones like white or cream for versatile pairing with throw pillows and curtains.
