Frequently Asked Questions

What is a macerating toilet and when should I use one?

A macerating toilet grinds waste and tissue before pumping it through small-diameter pipes, making it ideal for basement bathrooms, guest houses, or areas without traditional plumbing access. Simple Project's macerating toilets are designed for easy installation and reliable performance.

Are Simple Project smart toilets worth the investment?

Yes, Simple Project's smart toilets offer features like heated seats, bidet functions, soft-close lids, and energy-saving technology that enhance comfort and hygiene while reducing water usage. They're ideal for modern bathrooms and often provide long-term utility savings.
