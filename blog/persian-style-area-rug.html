Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between authentic Persian rugs and Persian-style rugs?

Authentic Persian rugs are hand-knotted in Iran using traditional methods passed down through generations, making them premium investments. Persian-style rugs replicate the classic designs and patterns but are often machine-made or produced in other countries, offering more affordable alternatives without sacrificing visual appeal.

How do I choose the right size Persian-style rug for my room?

Measure your space and consider furniture placement. For living rooms, choose a rug large enough for front legs of seating to rest on it. For bedrooms, place the rug under the bed or at the foot. Standard sizes include 5x8, 8x10, and 9x12 feet.

Are Persian-style rugs suitable for high-traffic areas?

Yes, Persian-style rugs are known for their durability and can handle high-traffic areas well. However, regular vacuuming and professional cleaning every 1-2 years help maintain their appearance and extend their lifespan.

How should I care for my Persian-style area rug?

Vacuum regularly without beater bars, rotate quarterly to ensure even wear, avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading, blot spills immediately, and schedule professional cleaning annually. Avoid harsh chemicals and always test cleaning solutions on inconspicuous areas first.
