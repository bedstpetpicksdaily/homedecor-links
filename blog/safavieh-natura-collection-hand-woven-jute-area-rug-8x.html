Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Safavieh Natura jute rug safe for homes with pets?

Yes, natural jute is a durable material that can handle pet traffic. However, jute can shed, so regular vacuuming is recommended to minimize loose fibers around pets.

Can I use the Safavieh Natura rug outdoors?

While jute is natural, it's not recommended for outdoor use as it can deteriorate in moisture and sunlight. This rug is designed for indoor spaces only.

How do I clean a jute area rug?

Vacuum regularly with a low suction setting and spot-clean spills immediately with a damp cloth. Avoid excess water and deep cleaning methods that can damage the natural fibers.

What room sizes work best for an 8x10 rug?

An 8x10 rug is ideal for large living rooms, bedrooms, or dining rooms. It works well in spaces at least 12x15 feet to provide proper proportions and comfort underfoot.
