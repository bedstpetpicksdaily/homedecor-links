Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install metal wall hanging sculptures?

Most metal wall sculptures require wall anchors, screws, or hooks appropriate for your wall type (drywall, plaster, or concrete). Use a stud finder for heavier pieces, follow manufacturer instructions, and ensure secure mounting before hanging your artwork.

What sizes of metal wall sculptures are available?

Metal wall sculptures range from small accent pieces (12-18 inches) to large statement art (36-60+ inches). Consider your wall space, room size, and existing furniture when selecting the appropriate dimensions for your home.

How do I clean and maintain metal wall art?

Dust regularly with a soft cloth or microfiber duster. For stubborn dirt, use a slightly damp cloth and mild soap. Avoid harsh chemicals. Inspect mounting hardware periodically to ensure continued security and structural integrity.

Will metal wall sculptures rust or tarnish?

Quality metal wall art is typically treated with protective coatings to prevent rust and tarnishing. Indoor pieces rarely rust, but humidity-prone areas like bathrooms may require additional care or pieces specifically designed for moisture resistance.
