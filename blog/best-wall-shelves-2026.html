Buying Guide: 3 Things to Look For

  • Weight Capacity: Consider what you plan to store. Heavy items like books require shelves rated for 50+ pounds, while lightweight décor can use shelves with lower ratings.
  • Material and Style: Choose materials that complement your room's aesthetic—wood for warmth, glass for elegance, or metal for industrial vibes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much weight can wall shelves hold?

Most quality wall shelves hold between 25-50 pounds when properly installed into studs. Always check the manufacturer's specifications and use appropriate anchors if you can't install into studs. Distributing weight evenly across the shelf extends its weight capacity.

Can you install wall shelves without drilling into studs?

Yes, you can use heavy-duty drywall anchors, toggle bolts, or monkey hooks to install shelves without studs. However, this method typically reduces weight capacity to 20-30 pounds per shelf. For heavier items, finding and installing into studs is always the most secure option.
