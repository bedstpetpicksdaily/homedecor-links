Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between pillar candles and jar candles?

Pillar candles are freestanding wax forms that require a separate holder for safety and decoration, making them versatile for different settings. Jar candles come in their own container, offering convenience, longer burn times, and built-in safety features while serving as complete decorative pieces.

How do I choose the right candle holder size?

Measure your candle's diameter and height, then select a holder that provides at least 1/4 inch clearance around the candle and extends slightly below the flame height. Consider your space proportions—larger rooms benefit from bigger holders, while compact spaces look best with petite designs to maintain visual balance.
