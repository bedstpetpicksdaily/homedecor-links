Frequently Asked Questions

How do I care for velvet pillow covers?

Most velvet pillow covers should be hand-washed or dry-cleaned to maintain their texture and color. Check the care label for specific instructions, as some covers may be machine-washable on a gentle cycle. Avoid bleach and tumble drying.

What sizes do velvet pillow covers come in?

Velvet throw pillow covers typically come in standard sizes like 16x16, 18x18, 20x20, and 22x22 inches. Check product specifications to ensure the covers fit your existing pillow inserts.

Are velvet pillow covers suitable for all rooms?

Yes, velvet pillow covers work well in living rooms, bedrooms, dens, and even offices. They add sophistication to any space and pair beautifully with other decorative accents like rugs, curtains, and wall art.

Can I mix velvet pillow covers with other textures?

Absolutely! Velvet pillow covers blend beautifully with other textures like linen, cotton, and leather. Mixing textures creates visual interest and depth in your home decor design.
