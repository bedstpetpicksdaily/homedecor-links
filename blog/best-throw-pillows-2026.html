Buying Guide: What to Look For in Throw Pillows

1. Fabric Quality and Feel

Choose fabrics that match your lifestyle—velvet for luxury, linen for a casual look, or performance fabrics if you have pets or kids. Consider how the fabric feels to the touch, as throw pillows are meant to be both decorative and comfortable.

2. Size and Shape

Standard throw pillows come in 16x16, 18x18, and 20x20 sizes, but you can also find lumbar rectangles and square options. Match the pillow size to your furniture—larger sofas can handle 20x20 pillows, while accent chairs work well with 16x16 sizes.

3. Fill Quality and Support

Look for high-density polyester or down-filled options that maintain their shape over time. Check that the insert is hypoallergenic and that covers are removable and machine washable for easy cleaning.

Frequently Asked Questions About Throw Pillows

How often should you replace throw pillows?

With proper care, quality throw pillows can last 3-5 years. However, you can extend their life indefinitely by simply replacing the covers and refreshing the inserts as needed, making them a cost-effective décor investment.

What's the difference between throw pillows and regular pillows?

Throw pillows are primarily decorative and smaller, designed to accent furniture and add style to a room. Regular pillows are larger and designed for sleeping, with different fill density and support requirements than decorative throw pillows.
