What is a macerating toilet and why would I need one?

A macerating toilet uses a pump system to break down waste, allowing installation in basements or areas without traditional plumbing access. Simple Project's macerating toilets are ideal for basement renovations, guest bathrooms, or fitness rooms where conventional toilet installation isn't practical.
