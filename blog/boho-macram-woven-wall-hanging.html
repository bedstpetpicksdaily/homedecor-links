Frequently Asked Questions

What size macramé wall hanging should I choose?

Choose based on your wall space: small (2-3 feet) for accent areas, medium (4-5 feet) for focal points, and large (6+ feet) for statement walls. Measure your space before purchasing to ensure proper proportions.

How do I clean and maintain a macramé wall hanging?

Gently dust with a soft brush or cloth monthly. For deeper cleaning, use a handheld vacuum on low suction or shake outdoors. Avoid water and direct sunlight to prevent fading and material deterioration.

What rooms work best for macramé wall hangings?

Macramé works beautifully in bedrooms, living rooms, entryways, and home offices. They add warmth to modern kitchens and create cozy atmospheres in reading nooks and meditation spaces.

Can macramé wall hangings work with modern decor?

Yes! Macramé pairs well with modern, minimalist, and eclectic styles. Choose neutral colors and simple designs for contemporary spaces, or embrace colorful bohemian patterns for maximalist interiors.
